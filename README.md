# Community Hero 🦸‍♂️🌍

> **An AI-powered hyperlocal platform that empowers citizens to report, verify, track, and resolve community issues with transparency, collaboration, and intelligent automation.**

---

# 📌 Overview

Community Hero is a civic engagement platform designed to bridge the gap between citizens and local authorities. It enables people to easily report infrastructure and public service issues such as potholes, water leakages, damaged streetlights, garbage accumulation, and other community concerns.

Using AI, geolocation, and community-driven verification, the platform streamlines issue reporting while providing complete transparency throughout the resolution process.

Our goal is simple:

> **Build smarter communities by making civic problem-solving collaborative, transparent, and data-driven.**
